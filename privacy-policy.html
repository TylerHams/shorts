Privacy Policy

This application is a personal content automation tool used to upload original short-form videos to TikTok through official TikTok APIs.

Information Accessed

The application may access basic account information required for secure authentication through TikTok OAuth, such as the authorised TikTok account identity. This is used only to connect uploads to the correct account.

How Information Is Used

Authentication tokens are used solely to upload original video content to the authorised TikTok account. The application does not sell, rent, share, or provide personal information to third parties.

Content Uploads

The application only uploads video files created and provided by the app owner. It does not scrape TikTok, automate likes, comments, follows, or other engagement activity.

Data Storage

Any access tokens used by the application are stored securely by the app owner and are used only for authorised account access.
