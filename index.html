ThoughtWarpShorts Automation Tool

This application is a personal content management tool used to help upload original short-form videos to authorised social media accounts through official platform APIs.

The app is used by the owner to manage and upload their own created video content. It does not scrape TikTok, automate engagement, or post content for unauthorised users.
